About the Club

The Bridgewater Bocce Club is a resident-led league for all skill levels, right here in our Viera community. Bocce is one of the world's oldest lawn games — and one of its most social. Two teams take turns rolling balls toward a small target ball called the pallino, earning points for landing closest. Simple to learn, endlessly fun, and perfect for any age or ability.

🎯 All Skill Levels Welcome

Complete beginners and experienced players alike will find a comfortable, encouraging home on our courts. We teach, we coach, and we cheer each other on.

🤝 Community First

More than a league, we are a gathering place. Expect post-game conversation, shared laughter, and genuine friendships built one roll at a time.

☀️ Outdoor & Active

Playing regularly outdoors with neighbors is one of the best investments you can make in your health, mood, and sense of belonging.

🏆 Friendly Competition

We keep score, we celebrate wins, and we congratulate good shots — whether they belong to your team or the other side.

How to Join

Getting started with the Bridgewater Bocce Club is easy. Follow these simple steps and you'll be rolling in no time.

1
Contact Club President Steve Schwartz

Reach out by phone at 786-255-6316 to express your interest. Steve will answer your questions and let you know about current openings.

2
Come to an Open Play Session

Before committing, we encourage you to show up on a Saturday morning and give bocce a try. No equipment needed — just bring yourself and your enthusiasm.

3
Register & Get Your Team Assignment

Once you're ready to join the league, Steve will walk you through the simple registration process and match you with a team (or help you form one with neighbors).

4
Show Up & Have Fun

That's it! The courts are here, the community is ready, and your spot on the court is waiting. Welcome to the club.
